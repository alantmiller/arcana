How It Works

Arcana encodes text into geometric symbols using a simple process: Your text is converted to UTF-8 bytes, then to hexadecimal, and finally each hex digit maps to a unique geometric symbol (‚ñ°, ‚ñ†, ‚óá, ‚óã, ‚ñ≥, etc.).

It's like a secret language hiding in plain sight - perfect for email signatures!
